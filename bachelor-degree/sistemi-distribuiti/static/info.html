Your bank, our bank.

Since when money was invented and then used, greediness corrupted our society. We want to embrace fully the ideals of the  Communist Party Manifesto and create the first bank where the money is shared between all the users. 

As the Manifesto reports, we must realize the "Centralization of money and credit in the hands of the state through a national
bank with state capital, and the suppression of all private banks and bankers". This is our opportunity.

Your money is our money, our money is your money.

Welcome to the Communist Party Bank!

How to use our site

You can navigate through our site with the navbar above. If you are here with a mobile device, click the star to show the menu. You can transfer money between all the users on the Transfer page, or cancel those transactions in the Divert page. In the home page you can see every account balance by searching its id (or clicking one inside the transaction table of someone else), while you can search accounts by name and/or surname in the Accounts page (where you can also create a new account, modify the existing ones, delete or re-activate an account; you can also activate the copy mechanism, with which you can copy two ids to paste in the Transfer page). You can sort any table by clicking the interested column title.

Friendly note

We are not serious. This is a university project and the money is fictional. CPBank doesn't stand for "Communist Party Bank", indeed CP are the initials (name-surname) of both the students who worked on this site. The idea of the communist bank came from the project requirements, which implied not using a login system and permitted everyone to change everyone's balance...just like in a communist utopia.
